## Find Missing Elements Of a Range
### Difficulty: _`Easy`_
---
### **Statement**
Given an array `arr[0...n-1]` of distinct elements and a range `[low, high]`, find all numbers that are in the range but not the array. The missing elements should be printed in sorted order.

---
### **Example 1**
- Input: `arr = [10, 12, 11, 15], low = 10, high = 15` 
- Output: `13, 14`

### **Example 2**
- Input: `arr = [1, 14, 11, 51, 15], low = 50, high = 55`
- Output: `50, 52, 53, 54, 55`
