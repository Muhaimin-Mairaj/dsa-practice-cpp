## Count Pairs With Absolute Difference Equal To K
### Difficulty: _`Easy`_
---
### **Statement**
Given an array `arr[]`and a positive integer `k`, the task is to `count` all pairs `(i, j)` such tha  
t `i < j` and absolute value of `arr[i] - arr[j]` is equal to `k`  

---
### **Example 1**
- Input: `arr = [1, 4, 1, 4, 5], k = 3` 
- Output: `4`

### **Example 2**
- Input: `arr = [8, 16, 12, 16, 4, 0], k = 4`
- Output: `5`
