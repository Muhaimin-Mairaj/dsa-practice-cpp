## Doubly Linked List Implementation
---
### **Statement**
The task is to implement the following functionalities of doubly linked list

- `Traveral`
  <ul><li> Forward Traversal of doubly linked list (Iterative and Recursive) </li></ul>
  <ul><li> Backward Traversal of doubly linked list (Iterative and Recursive) </li></ul>

- `Insertion`
  <ul><li> At the beginning </li></ul>
  <ul><li> At the end </li></ul>
  <ul><li> At the specific position </li></ul>

- `Deletion`
  <ul><li> From beginning </li></ul>
  <ul><li> From end </li></ul>
  <ul><li> From a specific position </li></ul>
  <ul><li> Of complete linked list </li></ul>

- `Searching`
  <ul><li> Find whether a given key exists in the list </li></ul>
